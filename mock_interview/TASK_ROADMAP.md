# Hiero Mock Interview Workspace

Welcome to the synchronized development pipeline. Each folder in this directory corresponds to a specific module of the Mock Interview System.

## Project Roadmap & Deadlines (2-Day Cycles)

| Folder | Module | Responsibility | Deadline |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **01_Question_Generation** | Question Logic | Creates interview questions from resume and JD | 2 Days |
| **02_Voice_and_STT** | Voice & Audio | Sets up audio capture and converts speech to text | 2 Days |
| **03_Answer_Analysis** | Logic Hub | Analyzes answers for mistakes and triggers feedback | 2 Days |
| **04_Feedback_Generator** | Report engine | Creates final report with scores and suggestions | 2 Days |
| **05_UI_Design** | Interface | Builds the interactive interview interface | 2 Days |
| **06_Integration** | Core Bridge | Connects all five pieces together | 2 Days |
| **07_Testing** | QC & QA | Tests the whole mock interview system | 2 Days |
| **08_Deployment** | Production | Deploys the system to production | 1 Day |

---

### How to use this space:
1. **Locate your assigned folder** based on your role.
2. **Commit your code and assets** directly into that folder.
3. **Keep track of the 2-day deadline** for your specific module.
4. Once completed, the **Integration** team will merge your work into the final product.

**Total Project Timeline: 9 Days from start to live.**
